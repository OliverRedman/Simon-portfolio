About Me

Simon is a freelance producer, musician and sound engineer with a growing involvement in musical direction. He has spent the majority of his formative years working on his craft weather that be his drumming or producing. Since a young age, Simon has been heavily involved in the church, on stage and behind the scenes. Throughout his career he has had the opportunity to take on and learn a variety of different roles such as backline technician, FOH/MON engineer, among many other roles.
