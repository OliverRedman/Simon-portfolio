About Me

Simon is a Mixing Engineer, Musician and Producer who is based in the UK.

His focus is in Commercial Pop and RnB music, spending the majority of his formative years honing his musical craft, specialising in drumming and music production. Life opened doors which allowed him to have further development opportunities and involvement in musical direction and show production.

Since a young age, Simon has been heavily involved in church productions, on stage and behind the scenes. Throughout his career, he has never been afraid to take on responsibilities, diversify, take on fresh challenges to stretch his ability, think outside of the box and expand his skill set by embracing a variety of roles such as: drumming, backline technician, FOH/MON engineer and working with live playback.

Networking has played an important part throughout his learning, allowing Simon to gain wisdom and knowledge from professionals throughout the music industry, creating opportunities to explore and learn in high-end production environments, which vary in range (from studio recordings to show rehearsals).
